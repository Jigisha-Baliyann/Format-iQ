Frequently Asked Questions

What is Format-iQ? +
Format-iQ is a free AI-powered tool that checks whether your research paper follows specific formatting standards such as IEEE.
Which formats are currently supported? +
Currently, Format-iQ supports IEEE formatting checks for research papers. Other formats such as ACM and Springer are planned for future updates.
How do I use Format-iQ? +
Simply upload your research paper in PDF or DOCX format, and Format-iQ will analyze it for compliance with the selected formatting standard.
Is Format-iQ free to use? +
Yes! Format-iQ is completely free for all users.

What types of formatting checks are performed? +
We check elements like font style, size, margins, page layout, heading structure, reference formatting, and more.
Will Format-iQ fix my paper automatically? +
Not in the current version. It will only detect and highlight formatting issues, but future versions may include auto-fixing features.
Can Format-iQ check for plagiarism or content quality? +
No. Our current scope is only formatting compliance. Content-based AI checks may be considered in future releases.
How accurate are the results? +
The results are generated using a set of formatting rules and validation logic. While highly accurate, manual review is still recommended.
What are the future enhancements planned? +
Upcoming features include:
  • Support for more publication formats (ACM, Springer, Elsevier, etc.)
  • AI-powered auto-fixing of formatting errors
  • Reference and citation checker
  • Template-based formatting suggestions
